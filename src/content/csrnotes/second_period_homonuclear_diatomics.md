## Five rules for forming MOs {#five-rules-for-forming-mos}

1.  Combining n AOs gives n MOs
2.  Only AOs of the correct symmetry will interact to form MOs
3.  The closer the AOs are in energy, the larger the interaction when MOs are formed
4.  Each MO is formed from a different set of AOs; AOs closer in energy to MO have higher coefficient in MO
5.  Sizes in AOs must be compatible for strong interaction in MO

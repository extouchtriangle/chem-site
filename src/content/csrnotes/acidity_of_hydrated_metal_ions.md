---
title: "Acidity of hydrated metal ions"
author: ["Easton"]
date: 2026-07-16
tags: ["csr"]
draft: false
pubDate: 2026-07-16
---

When metal ions are dissolved in water, they form coordinate covalent/dative bonds with nearby water molecules. This polarizes the O-H bonds in the water, causing them to become more acidic.

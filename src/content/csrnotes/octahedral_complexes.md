## MOs in octahedral complexes

First, we will assume that each ligand donates a single &sigma;-type valence orbital to the metal cation --- this could be a _p_ orbital or a hybrid with a lone pair.

First, classify each of the SOs by symmetry:
![](/ox-hugo/octahedral_sos.png)

Keeping in mind that the SOs can only overlap if they have the same symmetry, we can create the following MO diagram:
![](/ox-hugo/octahedral_mos.png)
The main orbitals that we'll consider are the 2e<sub>g</sub> and 1t<sub>2g</sub> orbitals on the metal (which are both antibonding).
